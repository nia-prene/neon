Title: Candy Pop
Developer: nia_prene
Release: 2023
Description: The classic Candy Cab era of shooters has come home to your NES in this cute and wacky bullet hell adventure. Two fledgling witches in The Witch Guild set out to join the most elite group of sorceresses, The Black Guardians. In order to join, they must pass the ultimate test, defeating  all the members of The Guardians in a row. Can you conjure the magic necessary to become part of The Guild's most famous coven?
